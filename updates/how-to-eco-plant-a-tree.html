How to ECO: Plant a Tree

What are trees?

What is the best way we can thank mother nature? Yup, that is right! One of the ways to be ECO is to plant a tree. Well, why are trees important? Trees give us oxygen, food, and is a beautiful. But the way we treat trees are unacceptable. Imagine burning our own resources and oxygen. We are creating carbon dioxide, the opposite ox oxygen. CO2 [carbon dioxide] comes from fire and smoke, ending up our oxygen supply

How to be ECO!

Simple:

  1. Plant More Trees
  2. Go Veg*
  3. Protest
  4. Don't Burn
  5. Farm Trees

*Why be veg? Being veg can reduce the need for meat. Meat needs to be cooked well so using electric cookers will not make it easy, ending up the use of wood.

It is time for us to make little changes for a big impact. Be ECO!
