Sustainability Week

We have a big week ahead. Sustainability Week is a big week with many events, hosted by the ECO-Committee. Many events for each weekday of the week

Monday

Monday features many school competitions but most importantly, the Sustainability Cities Workshop! ECO-Committee members can join an event where we discuss how to make a good city and plan for the future. Click here to join the workshop! and ask Miss Reid to join competitions

Tuesday

Another highlight of the week is the big Market. We are making swap shops, food stalls and many entertainment. All the money collected will be donated to the World Wildlife Funds!

Wednesday

The climate fresk is a simulation and activities time by the ECO-Committee. Each geography teacher will pick one person to join the fresk, per class. See what is happening!

Thursday

...and calling all public speakers to talk in this event. We need public speakers in our event. Please, contact Miss Reid to join

Friday

We are continuing to make more events, especially for friday. If you have any suggestions, ask Miss Reid to add on to our wonderful week!
