Sustainability Week - June 3rd to June 7

We have a big week ahead. Sustainability Week is a big week with many events, hosted by the ECO-Committee. Many events for each weekday of the week from June 3rd - June 7th. Please note that The Market is the only activity that is after school

Monday

Competitions & Workshop

Monday features many school competitions but most importantly, the Sustainability Cities Workshop! ECO-Committee members can join an event where we discuss how to make a good city and plan for the future.

Click here to join the Workshop, only for ECO-Committee members, teacher will give information
Contact Miss Reid* to join competitions.

Tuesday

The Market

Another highlight of the week is the big Market. We are making swap shops, food stalls and many entertainment. All the money collected will be donated to the World Wildlife Funds!

Market at Primary reception WSO, after school.

Wednesday

The Fresk

The climate fresk is a simulation and activities time by the ECO-Committee. Each geography teacher will pick one person to join the fresk, per class. See what is happening!

Your geography teacher will give the required information

Thursday

Public Speaking

We are introducing a public speaking competition for those who want their voice heard, all the wellington cluster will be invited to particapate from Year 5-13. Send All speeches to Miss Reid* to join.

Click here for info
Click here for criteria and joining
Public Speaking at Thursday 6th June 9:00AM-12:00PM Secondary Auditorium
*To contact Miss Reid, email s.reid1_wso@gemselearning.onmicrosoft.com
